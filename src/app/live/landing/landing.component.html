Our Travel Trailer Adventures!

Who are we?

We are Jim and Eloisa Garoutte and we love to travel!

"Moving me down the highway. Rolling me down the highway. Moving ahead so life won't pass me by. "

-Jim Croce

So, how did this whole travelling around the country in a trailer start?

We have always loved to travel and camp. In fact, we were avid tent campers for 25 years. But in 2011 we felt we were getting a bit old to crawl in and out of a tent so we took the plunge and bought our first travel trailer, a used 21 foot bunkhouse model made by R-Vision. It was the perfect trailer for us since we traveled with our daughter and since most of our trips were short weekend and holiday excursions. Then in April of 2018 Jim landed his dream job...a job that allows him to work remotely from anywhere in U.S. territory!

Our first long trip (as empty nesters) was done in September of 2018, right after we dropped our daughter off at college. We took a 2000 mile jaunt through parts of Texas, New Mexico, and Colorado. You can read about it here.

Every time we take a trip we will be adding information and photos about our experience. Hopefully it will be interesting enough that you continue to follow us on our journey!
